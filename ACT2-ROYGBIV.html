The Colors of the Rainbow

Red is the color of extremes.It represents intense love, seduction, aggression, risk, rage, and adventure.

Red

Orange is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light. It creates feelings of freshness, excitement and warmth.

Orange

Yellow is often considered the brightest and most energizing of the warm colors. It is related to joy and brightness. However, the color yellow can also represent dishonesty and cowardice.

Yellow

Green evokes feelings like revival, freshness, vitality and peace due to its connection to nature. This color aids in relaxation, as environments with green elements are peaceful and nurturing.

Green

Blue is the hue of dependability, safety, tranquility, and self-reliance. And it symbolizes trust and loyalty.

Blue

Indigo is a rich color between blue and violet on the visible spectrum, it's a dark purplish blue. It represents an inner awareness of spirituality, and allows for deep focus during meditation.

Indigo

Violet is associated with mystery, creativity, royalty and wealth, a combination that might explain its popularity in the cryptocurrency industry.

Violet
